About

As a driven leader with a passion for spearheading change, I seek to understand and improve crucial issues faced by the world today by meaningfully communicating and engaging with those I encounter. With my passions at the forefront of my professional career interests, I seek to better the lives of those around me through work at an organization that strives to make a global difference. A crucial step to any successful organization is effective and empowering marketing, displaying their mission to the public in a memorable way. With my emphasis on powerful and impactful communication, I consistently pursue opportunities to expand my online and offline marketing skill set. My ambitions ultimately aim to strategically manage marketing platforms while serving and bettering my community.
